Dear Amo,

Mi amor, Among billions of hearts, mine found yours. Among countless stories, ours became my favorite. Mi corazón, You've made me feel loved and cherished in two different timelines. It still breaks my heart knowing that I was the one who broke the beautiful bond we once shared. Yet when I look at where we are today, I can't help but believe everything happened exactly as it needed to. The younger version of me could never have loved you the way I love you now. I was still searching for myself, still trying to understand what love truly meant. Had life continued the way it was, I would have only hurt you more. Today, I thank God for changing my heart. People often say, "Right person, wrong time." But I don't think that's our story. I believe we met at the right time... so that life could shape us separately, only for God to bring us back together when we were finally ready to love each other the way He intended. I'm sorry for every tear I caused. I'm sorry for every moment I made you question your worth. You deserved a love that felt safe, and I'm grateful that today I can finally offer you that. This was supposed to be a simple love letter... Yet somehow, my heart decided it wanted to tell our story. Thank you for loving every version of me. The girl who didn't know herself. The woman who's still learning. And the one I'll continue becoming. You are my greatest treasure. My answered prayer. My safe place. My favorite hello. And the hardest goodbye, even if it's only for a little while. If my heart had a name... it would be yours. I choose you. In this lifetime. In every lifetime. Again. And again. Forever yours, Charmaine ❤️
